Stadium Information

Former Stadium: Highbury (Arsenal Stadium)

  • Location: Highbury, London
  • Opened: 6 September 1913
  • Closed: 7 May 2006
  • Capacity at Closure: 38,419
  • Architect: Archibald Leitch (original),
    C.W. Ferrier and W. Binnie (1930s redevelopment)
  • Notable Features: Art Deco East and West Stands, "Clock End"
  • Redevelopment: Converted into Highbury Square,
    a residential complex, preserving parts of the original structure

Current Stadium: Emirates Stadium

  • Location: Holloway, London
  • Opened: 22 July 2006
  • In Use Since: 2006–present
  • Capacity: 60,704
  • Surface: GrassMaster
  • Structural Engineer: BuroHappold
  • General Contractor: Sir Robert McAlpine
  • Owner/Operator: Kroenke Sports & Entertainment
  • Plan stadium renovation Could be completed by 2028
